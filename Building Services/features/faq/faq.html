Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and booking process

How do I book a service?

You can book a service through our website by clicking on the "Book a Service" button, selecting your desired service, choosing a date and time, and completing the booking form. You can also call us directly to schedule a service.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. During the booking process, you can enter your address to check if we service your location.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on the type of service, size of the property, and specific requirements. We provide free estimates after assessing your needs. You can get an approximate quote using our online booking form.

Are your service providers verified?

Yes, all our service providers undergo a thorough verification process including background checks, license verification, and skill assessment. We only work with qualified professionals.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled service. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.

How can I provide feedback or rate a service?

After your service is completed, you'll receive an email with a link to rate your experience and provide feedback. You can also log into your account and rate past services from your dashboard.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer emergency maintenance services for urgent issues like plumbing leaks, electrical problems, and other emergencies. Call our emergency line for immediate assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. Online bookings require payment authorization, but you only pay after the service is completed to your satisfaction.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, feel free to contact us directly.
